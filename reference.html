menu_book Rules at a Glance

The Four Pools

  • Body — physical wounds, hauling, climbing.
  • Mind — focus, signals, anomalies.
  • Soul — ghosts, omens, prayer.
  • Oxygen — fuel for everything. Spent to explore, fight, push tests.

If any pool hits 0, you fall to that kind of damage. Run out of Oxygen and you suffocate.

Character Creation

  1. Roll 3d6 each for Body, Mind, Soul. Roll 12 + d6 for Oxygen.
  2. Roll 15 + d6 credits.
  3. Roll a Mission, a Faction, a starting Weapon and starting Kit item (d6 each).
  4. Take the Base map.

Attribute Tests

Roll d6 + bonuses. Meet or beat the threshold for the pool tested.

Pool currentTarget

You may spend 1 Oxygen to add +1 to any test after seeing it.

Combat

  • To hit: roll d6, compare to enemy points (lower points = easier).
  • Damage: roll the weapon die. Route enemy damage to the listed pool.
  • Push: spend 1 Oxygen for +1 to an attack.
  • Flee: take the enemy's damage once; the room becomes a fled room (-1 to extraction later).
  • Armor: Hardsuit plating absorbs d4 Body per physical hit.

Room Procedure

  1. Spend 1 Oxygen to enter.
  2. Roll 2d6 on Room Shapes.
  3. Roll d4 on Doors (unless shape fixes it).
  4. Roll d6 on the Room Table for the encounter.
  5. Resolve. Mark safe or unsafe as appropriate.

Target & Extraction

  • Target room appears once you've explored 6+ rooms on a Quiet Salvage roll, or auto after 12 rooms (non-combat results).
  • Pass the mission test to grab the target cleanly; fail and take d4 of its pool.
  • Extract: spend Oxygen equal to (path − safe rooms − 1). Roll d6 + modifier, target 4+:
    +1 safe room found · +1 multi-door room · −1 fled · −1 living threat behind · −1 stayed after target
  • Fail extraction → roll on Extraction Complications.
  • Earn 15 threat points to scratch an Advancement.
